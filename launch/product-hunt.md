# AgentProof — Product Hunt Launch

## Tagline
Google Lighthouse, but for AI shopping agents

## Description
AI agents (ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, Perplexity) are the new storefront. But 87% of ecommerce stores are invisible to them.

AgentProof scans any ecommerce URL and generates an Agent Readiness Score (0-100) showing how well AI shopping agents can discover, interpret, and recommend your products.

## What we check:
- Schema & Structured Data (JSON-LD Product markup)
- Product Data Quality (descriptions, images, specs)
- Protocol Readiness (UCP, ACP, MCP support)
- Merchant Center Signals (returns, shipping, reviews, sitemaps)
- AI Discoverability (robots.txt for GPTBot/ClaudeBot, SSR content)

## Key findings from scanning 41 top ecommerce brands:
- Average score: 31/100
- Only 2 stores scored above 60
- Most stores are blocking AI crawlers without knowing it

## Pro features:
- Auto-generated copy-paste fixes for every issue
- Protocol file generator (UCP, MCP, robots.txt)
- AI Agent Simulation — see how agents would respond to shopping queries about your store
- Industry benchmark comparison

Free to scan. Pro plan for fixes and monitoring.
